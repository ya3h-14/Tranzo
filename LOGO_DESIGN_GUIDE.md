# TRANZO - Brand Identity & Logo Design Brief

## 1. Brand Overview
**Project Name:** TRANZO
**Industry:** Industrial Logistics & Heavy Mobility Solutions
**Tagline:** "Mobility Solutions That Build Our Nation"
**Core Values:** Strength, Reliability, Massive Scale, and Industrial Growth.

## 2. Design Philosophy
The logo must represent the **power and movement** of India's industrial backbone. Unlike standard delivery apps, TRANZO is built for **Heavy Machinery and Commercial Fleets**. The design should feel "Heavy-Duty" but digitally sophisticated.

## 3. Visual Elements & Vehicle Integration
*   **Primary Icon:** A bold, geometric letter **"T"**.
*   **Vehicle Symbolism (The "Truck/Crane" Element):**
    *   **The Grille:** The vertical bar of the "T" can be styled to resemble a heavy truck's front grille or a sturdy industrial pillar.
    *   **The Boom:** The horizontal bar of the "T" can subtly mimic the extending arm (boom) of an industrial crane.
    *   **The Silhouette:** A minimalist side-profile silhouette of a "Tata Ace" or a "Heavy Hauler" integrated into the "T" shape or following the wordmark.
*   **Movement:** Use negative space to create "road lines" or "tire tracks" moving forward.

## 4. Color Palette
*   **TRANZO Amber (#F59E0B):** High-visibility safety orange/yellow. Represents construction, energy, and road safety.
*   **Industrial Charcoal (#0F172A):** Deep, powerful black/grey. Represents steel, asphalt, and durability.
*   **Safety White (#FFFFFF):** For clean contrast on dark backgrounds.
